About me

Hi! My name is Rahul Gupta & I created this House Price Prediction website with the help of a Linear Regression Algorithm! I used Real estate database of Metropolitan cities like Mumbai, Delhi & Bangalore for creating the Machine Learning Model to predict the prices of property in different locations of respective cities.

A user can write the "Area (in square feet)", select the number of "BHK" and "Bathroom", and at last, choose the preferred "Location" and click on the "Predict" button. After clicking, the aforementioned ML model will predict the price of the house as per the data submitted by the user.
